About

I am a Master's student in Philosophy at the University of Houston. Beginning in the Fall, I will be a PhD student in the Department of History and Philosophy of Science at the University of Cambridge where I am generously funded by the Gates Cambridge Scholarship. My interests lie primarily in the philosophy of articial intelligence, general philosophy of science, cognitive science, pragmatism, feminist philosophy, and epistemic normativity. I'm also interested in machine learning's role in our epistemic systems, especially with respect to public discourse about science and politics. When I'm not doing philosophy I typically think a lot about third-wave coffee and music.
